About Agnes

A guide for the journey back to yourself

Every woman I work with is capable of an extraordinary relationship — with herself first, and then with the people she loves.

My Story

Why I do this work

I'm Agnes Mariadas, and I work with married women who feel like they've lost touch with themselves inside their own relationships — women who give so much to their marriage, their families, and everyone around them that their own voice gets quieter every year.

My approach starts inward, not outward. Before we work on a relationship, we work on the woman in it — her self-awareness, her sense of worth, the power she may have set aside without noticing. From that foundation, real and lasting change in a marriage becomes possible.

I believe coaching should feel like sitting with someone who truly sees you — not a checklist, not a script, but a steady, honest space to become who you already are underneath it all.
